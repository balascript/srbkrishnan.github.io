Greetee - Android Weather App

In a busy day, getting ready to work and travel planning is tiring. We forget to take care of ourselves from outside conditions. This is because of the lack of awareness of weather conditions and traffic conditions outside before we leave. Although there are plenty of apps available for the same reason, we fail to open them and plan. Greetee comes for save. Greetee suggests its user to do something before he/she leaves home for work or any event in his/her calendar. This app finds the weather condition outside suggests what to wear and also expected time to reach the desired place he/she planning to visit and its weather. This helps the user to be cautious of outside weather and possibly traffic conditions.

  • Service: Android Core App Development
